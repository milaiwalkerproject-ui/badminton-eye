# BadmintonEye Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-09**

BadmintonEye is designed with privacy first. All match data is processed on your device. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

---

## Data Processing

BadmintonEye processes all data on-device. No match scores, rally data, or personal information are transmitted to external servers operated by the developer.

---

## Camera

BadmintonEye uses your iPhone camera exclusively to power the HawkEye AI shuttlecock detection feature during active matches. The camera feed is analyzed in real time on your device and is never stored externally or sent anywhere. Camera access requires your explicit permission, which iOS requests via a standard prompt (NSCameraUsageDescription).

You can revoke camera permission at any time in Settings > BadmintonEye > Camera.

---

## iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, match history may sync to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is stored in your own iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > BadmintonEye.

---

## Apple Watch Connectivity

Match data syncs to your Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity (WCSession) framework. This sync happens directly between your iPhone and Watch over your local Apple ecosystem — no third-party servers are involved.

---

## Analytics and Crash Reporting

BadmintonEye does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. No usage data or behavioral telemetry is collected or sent to external services.

---

## Subscriptions

Hawk Eye Pro is an auto-renewing subscription product managed entirely by Apple and the App Store. BadmintonEye never receives, stores, or processes your payment information. All subscription billing and management is handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

---

## Children's Privacy

BadmintonEye does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by the app, there is nothing to delete or request.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of BadmintonEye change (for example, if crash reporting is added in a future update), this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Check this page for the latest version.
