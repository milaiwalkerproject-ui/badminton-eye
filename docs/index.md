# BadmintonEye

AI-powered badminton score tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch.

BadmintonEye uses on-device HawkEye AI to detect shuttlecock position and track match scores in real time — no manual input needed. Full Apple Watch integration keeps your score on your wrist during play.
