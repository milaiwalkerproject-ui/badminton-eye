BadmintonEye 2.0 — our biggest update yet.

WHAT'S NEW

Live Score Widget
See the current game score on your Lock Screen or Home Screen without opening the app. The widget updates live during a match and supports both small and medium sizes.

7-Day Free Trial for Hawk Eye Pro
New subscribers get a full 7-day free trial before any charge. Try AI line calls, multi-angle analysis, and the Live Score widget at no cost — cancel anytime from your Apple ID settings.

Offline Watch Sync
Score a match without a Wi-Fi or cellular connection and the Apple Watch stays in sync. Any score intents recorded offline are replayed to the Watch automatically when connectivity returns.

Onboarding & Accounts
Sign in with Apple to unlock iCloud sync across all your devices. Your match history, player profiles, and calibration settings follow you seamlessly. New users get a guided first-run experience that has you scoring your first match in under a minute.

Redesigned Paywall & Subscription Management
The Hawk Eye Pro upgrade screen has been rebuilt from the ground up — clearer pricing, an honest feature comparison, and a prominent yearly plan that saves you 50%. Terms of Service and Privacy Policy are now directly accessible from the paywall, satisfying App Store guideline 3.1.2(a).

Share Card Export
Tap the share button at the end of any match to generate a polished scorecard image — perfect for team chats, social posts, or keeping a personal record. The renderer handles singles and doubles layouts automatically.

Custom Scoring Formats
Build your own game format in the new Scoring Format Builder: set target points, enable or disable deuce, choose service rules, and save the format for reuse. Mid-match format switching now works correctly across all game phases.

IMPROVEMENTS
- Court calibration profiles are preserved across app restarts
- Watch companion app now requests HealthKit read/write permissions correctly via the WatchKit entitlement
- Scoring patterns chart is smoother on devices with ProMotion displays
- VoiceOver labels updated for the live-match tap zones and game info header

BUG FIXES
- Fixed a crash in ResultFusionService when a second-angle video contained no detectable frames
- Fixed service-rotation edge cases in 3x15 game formats when switching sides at games 3, 4, and 5
- Fixed an issue where abandoned matches could appear in the in-progress restore flow after relaunch
- Fixed layout clipping on the Match End scorecard on smaller iPhone screen sizes
- Fixed a crash that could occur when syncing match data to Apple Watch with non-ASCII player names or large payloads; the app now encodes safely and degrades gracefully on failure
- Improved WCSession reliability: score updates that previously could be silently lost when Watch connectivity was interrupted are now retried automatically via background context, with a double-delivery safeguard to prevent duplicate scores

As always, thank you for playing — and for every piece of feedback that made this release possible.
